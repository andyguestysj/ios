### What Is In SpriteKit?

* Images of	sprites, shapes	and	particles	
* Animations
* Physics	
* Audio/video	
* Visual Effects

### Basic Structure

* UIView/NSView	
  * SKView
  * Scene
    * Node
    * (+	Physics)
    * (+	Actions)
  
All items within a `scene` are `nodes`.
